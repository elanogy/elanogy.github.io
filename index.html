Elanogy in a nutshell


Elanogy is essentially an online personal development course provider, tailored specifically for 11-19-year-olds, with extra features that benefit teachers as well.

We aim to offer six subjects with our initial product release; survival skills, personal finance, nutrition, sociology & anthropology (moral, social and cultural), human rights, and mental health, with the goal of increasing our range in the future.
Each subject will have multiple levels, with each level constituting of several modules. Teachers will be given a subject guide of overviews of each level. They will then be able to choose the level for their pupils depending on their age, previous knowledge, and school/class focus and goals. Pupils will be able to progress through the levels.
Elanogy uses a ladder, experiential learning approach. Rather than assessing a pupil’s abilities to meet a certain objective, pupils will be guided through every step, repeating concepts until they are well understood. Pupils will be able to choose from a range of learning tasks. After gaining sufficient knowledge on the subject, they will be able to strengthen their understanding through a project. These projects will take pupils through the experiential learning process of trial and error, self-evaluation, feedback, and re-experimentation. They will be able to share their progress on the Communico platform and gain feedback from their teachers and classmates, as well as learn from their peers.
Pupils will earn points by completing tasks, working on their projects, posting progress updates, and interacting with other pupils. These points will benefit both the pupils and their schools. Pupils will compete for prizes and their schools will be able to use these points to claim technology resources, and consequently schools may consider supporting their less fortunate pupils.
Teachers can view real-time insights on student learning progress and engagement. Insight reports can be used to support teacher efficacy, and to be utilised in countries where schools are assessed officially.


Check Out Our Prototype!


We have set up the separate environments for students and teachers. The student environment includes functionality to access courses, complete tasks, and select projects to work on. The teacher environment includes the functionality to assign specific tasks to their students. The prototype also features Communico; a platform where students and their teachers can share updates, gain feedback, and learn from each other.
